**Architecture Decisions:**
- QUIC Library: msquic (Microsoft, MIT licensed, production-ready)
- Stream Multiplexing: Control (Stream 0), Mocap Data (Stream 1), Audio (Stream 2)
- Threading Model: Async I/O with dedicated worker threads (following NNG pattern)
- Datagram Strategy: Prefer datagrams for mocap (< 1280 bytes), fallback to reliable streams
- Backpressure: Queue-based buffering with 4MB default limit
