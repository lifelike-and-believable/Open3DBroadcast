# Product Management Templates

## Market Research Report Template

```markdown
# Market Research: [Topic]

## Executive Summary
[Key findings in 2-3 sentences]

## Research Objectives
- Question 1
- Question 2

## Methodology
- Sources consulted
- Time period covered
- Limitations/caveats

## Key Findings

### Finding 1: [Title]
[Details, evidence, sources]

### Finding 2: [Title]
[Details, evidence, sources]

## Competitive Analysis
[Comparison matrix or detailed breakdown]

## Market Trends
[Identified trends with supporting evidence]

## Implications for Open3DStream
[What this means for our product]

## Recommendations
1. [Action item with rationale]
2. [Action item with rationale]

## Sources
- [Source 1]
- [Source 2]
```

## Market Segment Profile Template

```markdown
### Segment: [Name]

**Description:**
[Who are they, what do they do]

**Size and Growth:**
- Market size estimate
- Growth rate and trends
- Geographic distribution

**Needs and Pain Points:**
- Primary needs
- Current solutions and gaps
- Budget constraints
- Technical capabilities

**Buying Behavior:**
- Decision makers
- Evaluation criteria
- Typical sales cycle
- Price sensitivity

**Fit with Open3DStream:**
- How well does the product fit?
- Unique value for this segment
- Go-to-market approach
- Priority level (High/Medium/Low)
```


## Competitive Analysis Template

```markdown
# Competitor: [Name]

## Company Information
- Founding date and history
- Funding status and investors
- Company size and locations
- Leadership team background
- Strategic partnerships

## Product Details
- Feature comparison matrix
- Pricing and licensing models
- Technology stack (if known)
- Platform support
- Integration capabilities
- Performance characteristics
- User experience observations

## Market Presence
- Target customer segments
- Case studies and reference customers
- Marketing messages and positioning
- Social media following and engagement
- Community size and activity
- Documentation quality

## Business Model
- Revenue streams
- Open-source vs. commercial mix
- Support and services offerings
- Ecosystem monetization
- Partnership models

## SWOT Analysis
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Opportunities
- Threats

## Our Response Strategy
[How we differentiate and compete]
```

```markdown
[What trends support growth?]

[What makes this hard?]

- Strengths: [What we do well]

- Differentiation: [Why us vs. others]

**Go-to-Market:**
[How would we reach this market?]

**Priority:**
[High/Medium/Low and why]

**Next Steps:**
[What research/actions needed?]
```

```markdown
- Timeline considerations
- Budget/resource limits
- Technical constraints
- Partnership dependencies

### Success Metrics
- How will we measure success?
- What metrics will we track?
- What constitutes MVP vs. full feature?

- Links to research findings
- Competitive examples
- User feedback sources
```
